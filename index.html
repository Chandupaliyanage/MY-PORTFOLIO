About Me

My name is Chandupa Disal Liyanage. I was born on January 27, 2003, and I am from Welliweriya. I attended Sri Bhodhi Vidyalaya in Gampaha for my primary education and Bandaranayake College in Gampaha for my secondary education. During my school years, I was an active member of the cadet team, the scout team, and the hockey team, with hockey being my favorite sport.

Academically, I achieved 1A, 6B, and 2C passes in my Ordinary Level exam. For my Advanced Level examination, I chose the technical subjects and obtained S passes in ICT and SFT. I am currently working at the iCET Institute in Panadura. My dream is to become the best traveler in the world.

I am a nature lover and deeply appreciate the beauty of the natural world. I hope that all humans will become nature lovers and work together to preserve our environment.

MOON

The Moon, Earth's only natural satellite, is a celestial body that orbits our planet at an average distance of about 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles). It is the fifth-largest moon in the solar system and plays a crucial role in influencing Earth's tides and stabilizing its axial tilt. The Moon's surface is marked by craters, mountains, and vast plains called maria. It has been a source of fascination and inspiration for humanity, and it was first visited by humans in 1969 during NASA's Apollo 11 mission.
